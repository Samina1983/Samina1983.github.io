Daman E Koh: The Most Beautiful Gardens in South Asia

A Beautiful Place to Visit

Daman-e-Koh is a picturesque garden ideally located in the center of the Margalla Hills. Daman-e-Koh is a combination of two Persian words that mean ‘foot hills’ when translated into English. Visitors can have a bird’s-eye view of Islamabad from Daman-e-Koh. Daman-e-Koh is almost 2400 feet above sea level and 500 feet above Islamabad. Nothing compares with the pleasure of looking at the beautiful capital city of Pakistan from a vantage point. The stunning view so enchants you that you lose track of time and want to spend maximum time there enjoying the mesmerizing view. People visit Daman-e-Koh to enjoy a lovely outing with friends and family. It is the perfect place for a picnic due to its panoramic view of Islamabad.
